Idea for random lights:
Decouple the odds with the number of lights. The odds are flat, for all level. (maybe they lower slowly a bit per level)
Instead of 50% chance for each light to be On, the game rolls a dice of 20 faces. The number of square lit is a ratio of that.
Roll 1: 1/20 of all lights are on
Roll 5: 5/20 of all lights are on
Roll 20: all of all lights are on
Benefits:
- Difficulty is flat
- No more guaranteed 100% odds over long time
- You have to watch it. You can't just wait.
